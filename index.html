Step 1 — Upload your data

Import your media history

Download your data export from each platform and drop it below. Everything is processed locally in your browser — the files never leave your device.

▶
YouTube
Watch history, liked videos, search queries
How to export ▶
  1. Go to takeout.google.com
  2. Click Deselect all
  3. Find and select YouTube and YouTube Music
  4. Click Next step → Create export
  5. Download the zip, open it, find watch-history.json
  6. Drop that file below

TikTok
For You Page history, liked videos, searches
How to export ▶
  1. Open TikTok → tap your Profile
  2. Tap the ☰ menu → Settings and Privacy
  3. Tap Account → Download your data
  4. Select JSON format → tap Request data
  5. Wait 1–4 days for TikTok to prepare it
  6. Download and drop the JSON file below
Drop TikTok export here
📸
Instagram
Liked posts, ads seen, search history
How to export ▶
  1. Open Instagram → tap your Profile
  2. Tap ☰ → Your activity
  3. Tap Download your information
  4. Select JSON format
  5. Choose Ads & businesses and Likes
  6. Request download, wait, then drop the JSON below

Live Capture

Capture your feed in real time

The Sophos bookmarklet lets you capture what's on your screen while browsing YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram — on any browser, on any device. One click sends it to your analysis.

Your Sophos capture tool
Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:
◉ Sophos Capture
(Drag the button above to your browser bookmarks bar — don't click it here)
1
Show your bookmarks bar — in Chrome press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac)
2
Drag the ◉ Sophos Capture button above onto your bookmarks bar
3
Go to YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram and browse normally
4
Click ◉ Sophos Capture in your bookmarks bar — it grabs what's visible and stores it locally
5
Come back to Sophos and click Analyze now — your captured content is included in the analysis
Works on mobile too
On iPhone or Android, open YouTube or Instagram in your mobile browser (not the app), bookmark the page, then replace that bookmark with the Sophos Capture link.
